This analysis reviewed various technology application cases to identify the latest AI Transformation (AX) trends in the manufacturing industry. As a result of the analysis, it was confirmed that AI is acting as a key driver for innovating productivity and efficiency on manufacturing floors.

The most prominent trends are 'Predictive Maintenance' and 'AI-based Quality Inspection'. Combining industrial IoT sensors with AI to predict equipment failures in advance and automatically detect defective products using computer vision technology are methods that directly contribute to cost reduction and quality improvement, and are becoming widespread. Furthermore, 'Digital Twin', evaluated as the pinnacle, is a technology that builds a factory identical to reality in a virtual space to simulate and optimize the entire process, drawing attention as the ultimate form of smart factories. Additionally, 'Energy Efficiency Optimization' utilizing AI is emerging as a significant opportunity factor for ESG management and achieving carbon neutrality goals.

In conclusion, for a successful AI transformation, it is important to start in areas where clear Return on Investment (ROI) is expected, such as predictive maintenance and quality management, and in the long term, establish an enterprise-wide roadmap that includes integration with core systems like ERP and the construction of digital twins.
