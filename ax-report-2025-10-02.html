This report, based on real-world cases, presents an implementation path that removes bottlenecks using factory telemetry, quantifies throughput and cost improvements through 8-12 week PoCs, and enhances planning quality and resilience with scenario-based digital S&OP that integrates demand, materials, and capacity into a single view. TrustEval과 RISE 프레임워크는 제조 현장의 GenAI 도입 시 공정성·안전성·프라이버시를 체계적으로 평가·관리하는 거버넌스 기준으로 유용하다. 단기 PoC(8~12주 텔레메트리), 디지털 S&OP 시나리오 운영, 보안 데이터 샌드박스 구축, VR 안전 모듈 파일럿, AI 평가 매트릭스 도입을 즉시 실행 과제로 권장한다. This report presents significant cases and frameworks from a manufacturing AX perspective. Southwire's equipment telemetry analysis led to throughput improvement and identified a potential saving of $3.5 million, while ITAMCO enhanced production and supply chain linkage with risk-embedded digital S&OP. DatModo is a platform that securely connects sensitive data with AI computations, making it directly applicable to supply chain collaboration and quality analysis. Furthermore, VR training for handling hazardous materials is highly effective in reducing safety accidents and improving proficiency. The TrustEval and RISE frameworks are useful as governance standards for systematically evaluating and managing fairness, safety, and privacy when introducing GenAI into manufacturing sites. Short-term PoC (8-12 week telemetry), digital S&OP scenario operation, secure data sandbox establishment, VR safety module pilot, and AI evaluation matrix adoption are recommended as immediate action items.
