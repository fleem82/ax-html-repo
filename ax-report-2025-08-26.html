Summary of Key Trends in Manufacturing AI Transformation (AX)

This analysis aims to identify key changes and opportunities by synthesizing the latest manufacturing AI technology trends. The analysis shows that manufacturing AI is rapidly spreading on-site, primarily focused on predictive maintenance and quality inspection. Technologies that proactively predict equipment failures with Industrial IoT (IIoT) sensor data and automatically detect subtle defects with computer vision have become a key driver for enhancing productivity and stability.

Recently, the role of AI shows a clear trend of expanding beyond detection to autonomous process control and generative design. This optimizes processes in real-time and drives fundamental innovations such as lightweighting and component integration from the product design stage, thereby creating new added value.

For successful AI adoption and enterprise-wide deployment, the challenges are also clear. Securing technologies where AI can flexibly respond to changes in production environments (domain adaptation) is crucial, and systematic data governance must be established through the convergence of IT and OT (Operational Technology) systems. To overcome the 'pilot's swamp' and achieve tangible results, a long-term approach based on flexible infrastructure such as hybrid edge-cloud and federated learning is essential.
