Summary of Key Trend Analysis for AI Transformation (AX) in the Manufacturing Industry

This summarizes the results of key trend analysis related to AI Transformation (AX) in the manufacturing industry. This analysis highlights the success factors and challenges of AI adoption from various perspectives, including not only technology but also leadership and human resources.



The most notable technology trend is the combination of Generative AI and 3D printing (additive manufacturing). This is driving innovation in product design and quality assurance, and AI is playing a key role in advanced decision-making support, such as complex demand forecasting, predictive maintenance, and intelligent scheduling in a make-to-order environment.



Multiple analyses commonly emphasize that the biggest key to successful AI transformation lies not in technology, but in 'people and organization'. Strong leadership commitment and the establishment of company-wide AI and data literacy are essential. Furthermore, the 'Human-in-the-loop' approach, where AI systems and human experts collaborate, and the cultivation of future-oriented smart factory personnel have emerged as important tasks.



In conclusion, AI transformation in manufacturing demands holistic innovation that goes beyond merely adopting new technologies. For success, a strategic approach encompassing technology, people, and processes is required, and the importance of Explainable AI (XAI) technology, which helps users trust and understand AI's judgments, is also expected to grow.
