Among the key trends, first, the scope of AI application is expanding enterprise-wide. It is trending to be linked with the entire value flow of the enterprise, from demand forecasting and inventory optimization (supply chain) to digital twin-based zero-defect (production), and the introduction of AI agents for on-site workers (workplace). This shows that AI is evolving through prediction (defect prediction) and optimization (process parameter optimization) to the stage of autonomy (unmanned automation, self-optimizing supply chain).
