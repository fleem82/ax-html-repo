그러나 성공적인 전환을 위해서는 체계적인 데이터 거버넌스 구축과 AI 도입 준비도 평가가 선행되어야 합니다. 동시에 AI 칩 공급망 리스크, OT(운영기술) 보안을 포함한 사이버 위협, 그리고 EU AI Act와 같은 규제 및 윤리 문제에 대한 전략적 대응이 핵심 과제로 부상했습니다.

따라서 성공적인 AX는 개별 기술 도입을 넘어 거버넌스, 보안, 규제를 포괄하는 전사적 관점의 접근이 필수적입니다.
According to the analysis of manufacturing AI transformation (AX) trends, AI technology is evolving beyond simple automation of production sites into intelligent autonomous operation.

Key application areas include AI robotics, predictive maintenance, and quality inspection utilizing Vision AI. Recently, generative AI and agent-type AI, combined with digital twin, are creating new opportunities to innovate not only design and production but also back-office work productivity.

However, for a successful transformation, the establishment of systematic data governance and an assessment of AI adoption readiness must precede. Simultaneously, AI chip supply chain risks, cyber threats including OT (Operational Technology) security, and strategic responses to regulatory and ethical issues such as the EU AI Act have emerged as key challenges.

Therefore, successful AX goes beyond the adoption of individual technologies and requires an enterprise-wide approach encompassing governance, security, and regulation.
