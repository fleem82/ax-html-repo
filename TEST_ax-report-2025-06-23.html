This analysis was conducted to identify the latest trends and key challenges related to AI Transformation (AX) in the manufacturing industry.

The analysis revealed a distinct trend where the scope of AI technology application is expanding beyond process innovation within smart factories to encompass the entire business, such as data-driven demand forecasting and decision-making. This indicates that AI is moving beyond being a mere productivity improvement tool and establishing itself as a strategic asset for companies.

These technological opportunities, coupled with the need for structural reform faced by traditional process industries such as steel and petrochemicals, further highlight the importance of AI transformation.

However, the biggest challenge for a successful transformation is the 'AI talent war'. Securing skilled talent has emerged as a core competency for companies, and to address this, an organizational and strategic approach is essential, such as establishing a centralized AI platform centered around a Chief AI Officer (CAO).

In conclusion, for a successful AI transformation in manufacturing, along with technology adoption, preparing for fierce talent competition and establishing an enterprise-wide AI strategy to strengthen organizational capabilities are paramount.
