As the AI Transformation (AX) of the manufacturing industry accelerates, a fundamental paradigm shift beyond mere technology adoption is being observed. Amid this trend, the key insights for manufacturing experts to consider are as follows:

First, 'Generative AI' is emerging as the core driver for implementing the 'Smart Factory'. The potential applications of Generative AI are expanding across various domains, including design, simulation, and quality control.

Second, for successful AI adoption, the underlying foundation supporting it is becoming more crucial than the technology itself. Establishing a 'data strategy' to secure and utilize high-quality data is essential, and the establishment of 'AI Governance' and a 'Trustworthy AI' framework to ensure the transparency and stability of AI operations is emerging as a key success factor.

In conclusion, a successful AI transformation in manufacturing can be achieved not merely through technology adoption, but through data-centric strategic innovation built upon a foundation of trust and governance.
