Analysis of AI Transformation (AX) in manufacturing indicates that AI is emerging as a key driver, revolutionizing the entire manufacturing value chain beyond mere fragmented technology adoption. In particular, attention should be paid to the following key trends and insights:

The most important trend is the advancement of 'Digital Thread Optimization' and 'Digital Twin'. This signifies that AI has reached a stage where it can perfectly connect the entire process, from product design to production, distribution, and service, with data, and simulate real-world factories through digital twins implemented in a virtual world to derive optimal operational solutions. Through this, manufacturing experts can maximize productivity and minimize downtime with predictive maintenance.

Furthermore, the popularization of 'data-driven decision-making' through 'Prompt Engineering' is notable. Now, even without being data scientists, field managers or engineers can ask AI questions in natural language (everyday language) and analyze necessary data to gain immediate insights. This will significantly enhance rapid and accurate on-site problem-solving capabilities.

From a long-term perspective, the importance of 'AI Governance' and 'AI Infrastructure' is emphasized. Establishing policies and management systems (governance) for the responsible and stable operation of AI technology is an essential prerequisite for successful AX.
