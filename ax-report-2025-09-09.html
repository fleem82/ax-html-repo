This document summarizes the analysis results aimed at identifying key trends in AI Transformation (AX) within the manufacturing industry. In conclusion, for a successful manufacturing AI transformation, it is essential to establish an integrated strategy that goes beyond adopting individual technologies, encompassing the entire value chain from production to supply chain, based on cloud infrastructure.
